# US-008: Automatic identification and mapping of employee studies to EQF/MECES

> **Epic:** [EPIC-03](../epics/EPIC-03.md) — EQF / MECES Study Mapping  
> **Priority:** P1 (Must)  
> **Story Points:** 5  
> **Sprint:** Sprint 3  
> **Status:** New  
> **Business Requirement:** BR-EQF

## Description

As a **Manager/Account Lead**, I want the system to automatically identify the academic qualifications of each employee from Workday and map them to their corresponding EQF and MECES level, so that the employee's education level is standardised and comparable across the organisation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Given an employee has education data in Workday (degree, institution, field of study), when the mapping process runs, the system matches the qualification against the EQF/MECES table and assigns the corresponding EQF level (1–8) and MECES level
- [ ] The assigned EQF and MECES levels are stored as part of the employee's profile and are visible in search results and dashboards
- [ ] When a qualification cannot be automatically matched, it is flagged as 'Pending mapping' and surfaced to the administrator for manual resolution
- [ ] The mapping is re-evaluated automatically when the employee's education data in Workday is updated
- [ ] The EQF/MECES level is available as a search and filter criterion in candidate searches
