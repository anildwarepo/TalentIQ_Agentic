# EPIC-15: Query History

> **Priority:** P1 (Must)  
> **Sprint:** Sprint 4  
> **Business Requirement:** BR-14  
> **Total Story Points:** 2

## Summary

Store and display per-user query history organized by date, allowing reuse and review of previous searches.

## User Stories

| Story | Title | Points | Sprint | Status |
|-------|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| [US-045](../user-stories/US-045.md) | Query history and relaunch previous searches | 2 | Sprint 4 | New |

## Dependencies

- **Upstream:** EPIC-04 (search engine generates the queries to save)
- **Downstream:** None

## Acceptance Criteria (Epic Level)

- Per-user history organized by date
- Previous queries and results viewable
- One-click relaunch of previous searches
