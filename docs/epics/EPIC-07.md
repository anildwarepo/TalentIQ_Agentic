# EPIC-07: Manager Dashboards (People Analytics)

> **Priority:** P1 (Must)  
> **Sprint:** Sprint 5  
> **Business Requirement:** BR-05  
> **Total Story Points:** 20

## Summary

Dashboards and reports summarizing certifications, skills, languages, and EQF/MECES levels across the population for strategic planning. Includes RFI coverage heat maps, My Team views, visual indicators for CV freshness and certification status, and aggregated reports.

## Dependencies

- **Upstream:** EPIC-01 (Workday data), EPIC-03 (EQF/MECES levels), EPIC-04 (search results), EPIC-06 (certification data)
- **Downstream:** EPIC-08 (notifications triggered from dashboard insights)

## Acceptance Criteria (Epic Level)

- Population-wide dashboard with filterable views by certifications/skills/languages
- RFI coverage heat map (green/amber/red) with real-time updates
- My Team view restricted to manager's scope
- Visual indicators for CV freshness and certification status
- Drill-down from dashboard to employee profile
- Reports accessible with permissions
